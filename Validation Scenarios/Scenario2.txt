Objective: Verify that the STT rejects continuous background noise using dynamic thresholding, and that complex queries utilize the sentence-by-sentence streaming TTS to reduce perceived latency.

Test Execution:

    Play background coffee-shop chatter near the robot to test the calibrate_noise_floor() logic.

    Speak clearly over the noise: "Hey AURA, explain the history of robotics in three long sentences."

Expected Outcome:
