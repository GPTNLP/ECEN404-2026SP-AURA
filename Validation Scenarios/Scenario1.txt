Scenario 1: Hardware & Kinematic Validation (The Physical Layer)

    Objective: Verify that the Jetson accurately translates directional movement commands into synchronized bipedal servo movements.

    Test Execution: 1. Send a MOVE:forward command.
    2. Wait 3 seconds, then send a MOVE:backward command.
    3. Wait 3 seconds, then send a MOVE:stop command.

    Expected Outcome: 1. The forward command should trigger the alternating bipedal gait loop instantly.
    2. The backward command should reverse the leg/arm swing polarity without stuttering.
    3. The stop command should immediately halt the gait and return all four servos to their 90-degree flat home positions. The ESP32 must echo ACK:MOVE:stop.
