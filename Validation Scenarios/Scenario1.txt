Scenario 1: Integrated Hardware & Edge Actuation

    Objective: Verify that the Jetson translates intent into simultaneous physical action, voice feedback, and peripheral control without latency blocking.

    Test Execution: 1. Send a web command to activate the camera in detection mode.
    2. Speak the voice command: "AURA, please move forward."
    3. Send a web command MOVE:stop.

    Expected Outcome: 1. The camera immediately starts streaming to the UI.
    2. The STT loop correctly routes the intent as movement, passing it to the ESP32 to trigger the bipedal gait. Simultaneously, the TTS service outputs "Moving forward" without interrupting the motor serial link.
    3. The stop command immediately halts the gait and returns all four servos to their 90-degree flat home positions, bypassing the voice queue.
